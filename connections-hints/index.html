What is Connections?

Connections is a daily word puzzle where players must find 4 groups of related words. Each group shares a hidden connection, and finding them all solves the puzzle.

Strategy Tips

  • Start with obvious groups first
  • Look for shared themes like food, animals, or colors
  • Avoid random guessing early
  • Think about categories, not individual words
