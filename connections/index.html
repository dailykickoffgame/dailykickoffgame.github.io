How to Play

Your goal is to discover 4 sets of connected words. Each set contains exactly 4 words that share a category, pattern, or common idea. Choose carefully: too many wrong guesses and the round ends.

Why Players Love Connections Games

Word grouping games combine logic, vocabulary, and deduction in a format that is easy to start but hard to master. They are ideal for players who enjoy short daily brain challenges, puzzle streaks, and games that can be shared with friends.

Tips to Improve

  • Look for obvious categories first, such as colors, animals, places, or objects.
  • When stuck, try spotting words with similar context instead of surface similarity.
  • Use process of elimination after one or two groups are solved.
  • Replay daily to improve pattern recognition and puzzle speed.
