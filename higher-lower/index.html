How to Play

You will see one football stat with its value and a second football item with the value hidden. Guess whether the hidden stat is higher or lower than the current one. Each correct answer extends your streak.

Why This Game Is Fun

Higher or Lower games are simple to understand but hard to master. The football angle makes each round more memorable, especially for players who enjoy stats, rankings, player value comparisons, and quick decision-making.

Tips to Improve Your Score

  • Start by trusting categories you know well, such as goals, appearances, or market value.
  • Pay attention to player age, club level, and competition strength when estimating values.
  • Do not rush obvious guesses. Mid-range values often create the hardest decisions.
  • Play multiple times to improve your intuition for football stats.
